Caring for your Pets

To get your dogs coat extra shiny, wash your dog every two weeks, and brush 3/4 times a week


To pick up your cat, place one hand behind the front legs and another under the hindquarters. Lift gently.


Pet rabbits can be taught to respond to commands using positive reward-based training
